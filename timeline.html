History of IOSD

From Where It All Started

SMALL TEAM WITH BIG DREAMS

A Core-Team of 5 Android Techies started its operations to promote Software Development across different streams in College

FIRST ORIENTATION

An orientation was organised to let students know more about the student-organisation and the things that they will experience in the society and get to learn about in software development. An introductory session on software development was also conducted, which catered to many doubts of the audience and gave a basic outline on software development.It received an overwhelming response from students of all streams in the college and had witnessed the maximum footfall ever seen for a club orientation.

ERA OF PROJECTS

Members of IOSD along with their mentors completed various projects in different fields. 17 web based projects and 6 android projects were completed by 70 students.

SYNERGY'17

SYNERGY'17 went on to be the greatest Tech-Fest North India had ever witnessed. Various competitons were held with emulous students participating from all across India vying for the cash prize. Big Tech giants like Cisco were the Title Sponsors. HackDTU, a 24 hour Hackathon bacame a huge attraction for all Developers across Delhi-NCR.

GENESIS OF IOSD

After achieving great Heights in the first year itself, we underwent massive expansion in various colleges across Delhi-NCR and led to the formation of International Organisation of Software Developers.

Inception of Largest student
run Organisation in Delhi-NCR

We came , we saw , we conquered. Wherever we established a student chapter, it didn’take us much time to grow into the biggest chapters of each college. It dosen’t take students much time to realise what’s best for their future.

SYNERGY 2.0

Synergy 2.0 turned out to be a bigger fest than we could have thought of with thousands of students participating in different competitions like Code-in-Less or HACKDTU or getting an internship from various budding start-ups or established companies via our Internship fair.

E-learning Resource portal

At IOSD we believe the learning should never stop and should be along a smooth curve. So to emphasise on that we have developed an online resource portal on which everything needed for perfecting your ability to code,from E-books to online lectures will be available throughout the year which will help you get through different stages of your exams.

First step outside Delhi-NCR

The first time we ever stepped outside the state was for IIIT-GWALIOR to include another chapter with wide open arms into the IOSD family. We hosted the orientation at their auditorium in March and have been getting phenomenal response ever since.

Tech Day Spring Edition 2018

TechDay Spring Edition 2018 marks the beginning of this quarterly event in which workshops are organised by various hot industries and several coding and gaming competitions are conducted too. Professional like the co founder of Coding Blocks, Prateek Narang conducted workshops in this event.

Orientation Tour 2018

International Organisation of Software Developers is one big family, which started with one regime at one of the premier institutes in India is now across the subcontinent. Its been outstanding last academic session and at #IOSD we look forward to another outstanding season with new members that join our mission to promote Software development culture in India.

IOSD Annual Meetup 2018

Teaching newbies, learning from pros, taking responsibility, feeling proud when people deem an IOSD event a phenomenal success, all of it is part and parcel of the journey for us.

IOSD Hackathons Spring 2019

In Spring 2019, we bought a whole lot of opporunities to students by conducting seven hackathons in various colleges across three states. Students learned various skills and created projects which were beyond the imagination. Hackathons - hackDTU ,hackJU ,hackBPIT ,hackGTBIT ,hackMAIT ,hackMSIT, hackSRMIST

IOSD Summer of Code 2019 (ISOC'19)

IOSD Summer of Code 2019 (ISOC'19) is an open source competition organise during the summers. Students are allocated projects and divided in teams and under expert guidance complete the projects. It concludes with prize distribution to top contributors.
